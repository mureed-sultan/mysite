What are we ?

JakTech Solution is a boutique creative agency built around the best people and networks available , offering a wide variety of creative and art buying services for campaigns of all sizes.

Our aim is to turn business strategy into powerful and meaningful design experiences that drive business growth.

Such is our holistic outlook at VM, we thrive on offering an array of production solutions in digital, photography, print, film and video, so we can be your go-to facilitator.

Why we work together - David Ellis & Nick Bayat

David is a highly experienced , experimental and versatile photographer and Nick has studied and followed a lifelong career in Graphic Design. The harmony and mutual respect of these two skill sets, alongside their individual outlooks coming together through many projects, has made a very effective collaboration in producing great work between them.

David’s eye for an image compliments Nick’s desire for order and structure in design.

David can be instinctive and raw in his thinking and creativity, often initiating concepts and aspirations, which, in turn can trigger chain reactions in the effective research and development mindset, characteristic of Nick’s communication.

The research and awareness of an idea then allows David to thrive within the excellence of making the ‘picture perfect’ photographic contribution. This shared interaction has often led them to beautiful multi-faceted routes that work across a spectrum of brand requirements and demands.

This, in turn, delivers maximum value to our clients.
